Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation must benefit all layers of society to ensure equitable growth and social stability.

Sustainability

Sustainability is crucial for supporting economic development.

Infrastructure

Improving access to healthcare and education in rural areas.

Empowerment

Developing a skilled workforce is central to fostering innovation and competitiveness.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"When we want to help the poor, we usually offer them charity. Most often we use charity to avoid recognizing the problem and finding the solution for it. Charity becomes a way to shrug off our responsibility. But charity is no solution to poverty. Charity only perpetuates poverty by taking the initiative away from the poor. Charity allows us to go ahead with our own lives without worrying about the lives of the poor. Charity appeases our consciences."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
